Account Security and Access Policy

## 1. Password resets
Customers can reset their password from the login page via a reset link emailed to their registered address. 
Reset links expire after 30 minutes. After 5 failed login attempts, the account is temporarily locked for 15 minutes.
After 10 failed attempts within an hour, the account is locked until the customer completes a password reset.

## 2. Two-factor authentication (2FA)
2FA is mandatory for all accounts. Supported methods are authenticator apps (recommended) and SMS. 
If SMS codes are not arriving, customers should verify their phone number on file.
Support can temporarily switch the account to email-based codes after identity verification.

## 3. Lost 2FA device
A customer who loses access to their authenticator app (for example, after getting a new phone) must complete identity re-verification.
This includes a government ID upload plus a confirmation code sent to the registered email. 
Re-verification is reviewed within 1 business day. Backup codes generated at 2FA setup can be used to log in immediately and re-enroll a new device without re-verification.

## 4. Suspicious activity and account compromise
If suspicious activity is detected (login from a new country, unusual transaction pattern), an alert email is sent and we may proactively freeze the account. 
Customers who believe their account is compromised should use the "Freeze my account" link in any security alert email or contact support.
This will immediately: (a) lock the account, (b) invalidate all sessions, and (c) block outbound transfers pending review. 
Compromised-account reviews are prioritized and completed within 4 business hours.

## 5. Account recovery without email access
If a customer no longer has access to their registered email address, recovery requires identity verification with government ID and may take up to 3 business days. 
Support cannot change the email address on an account based on a request alone.

## 6. Account deletion and data
Customers may request permanent account deletion at any time. 
Deletion is completed within 30 days, after which personal data is removed except records we are legally required to retain.
Transaction records are retained for 7 years under financial regulations. 
Deletion cannot be reversed after the 30-day window closes; within the window, the customer can cancel the request by logging in.

## 7. Data access requests
Customers may request an export of their personal data and transaction history from the Privacy page; exports are delivered within 72 hours as a downloadable archive. 
Statements for specific date ranges can also be downloaded directly from the Documents page.
