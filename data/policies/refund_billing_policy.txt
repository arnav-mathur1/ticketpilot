Refund & Billing Policy

## 1. Subscription refund window
Customers may request a full refund of any subscription payment within 14 calendar days of the charge date, no questions asked. 
Refund requests made between 15 and 30 days after the charge are eligible for a prorated refund covering the unused portion of the billing period. 
Requests made more than 30 days after the charge date are not eligible for a refund except where required by law or in cases of verified billing error.

## 2. Duplicate and erroneous charges
If a customer is charged more than once for the same billing period, the duplicate charge is refunded in full within 5–7 business days of the customer's report being verified. 
Verified billing errors (charges that do not match the published plan price, undisclosed fees, or incorrect proration) are refunded in full regardless of when they are reported.

## 3. Proration on plan changes
When a customer upgrades mid-cycle, the unused value of the current plan is credited against the new plan's price, calculated to the day. 
Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle; no mid-cycle credit is issued for downgrades.

## 4. Cancellation
Cancelling a subscription stops all future charges but does not automatically trigger a refund of the current period. 
If a customer reports being charged after a confirmed cancellation, the post-cancellation charges are refunded in full, and the customer should receive the refund within 5–7 business days.

## 5. Fees
The standard plans are Basic ($9.99/month), Plus ($19.99/month), and Premium ($29.99/month). 
There is no charge for processing fees, setup fees, or cancellation fees on standard consumer plans. 
Any fee not listed in this section appearing on a consumer statement should be treated as a billing error (see Section 2).

## 6. How refunds are issued
Refunds are issued to the original payment method only. 
If the original payment method is closed or expired, the customer must contact support to arrange an alternative, which may require identity verification. 
Refund processing time after approval is 5–7 business days for cards and 1–3 business days for bank transfers.

## 7. Invoices and tax documents
Customers can download invoices from the Billing page. 
Invoices including a company name and tax ID can be generated by updating the Billing Profile before requesting the invoice; support can regenerate past invoices with updated details on request.
