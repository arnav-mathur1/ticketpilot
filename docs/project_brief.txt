TicketPilot Overview

Problem:
Support services at financial companies receive high volumes of tickets with varying urgency. Manually triaging every ticket is a slow process.
However, fully automating the replies is unsafe because the industry is highly regulated.

Solution:
TicketPilot is a deployable 2-fold solution.
1. Triage Agent: classifies the incoming tickets (category, urgency), drafts a reply, and attaches a confidence score.
High confidence, non-sensitive tickets get an auto-draft reply released automatically.
Low-confidence or sensitive tickets (such as involving money, disputes), are routed to a human.

2. Policy Q&A Agent (RAG): answers natural-language questions from a policy/FAQ document list, with citations to the exact document/section.

Users:
1. Support agents (the humans on the Triage Agent side): they sit in front of the approval dashboard, see the tickets the agent pre-sorted, and click approve/reject on the drafted replies.
2. Customers/internal staff (the humans on the RAG Agent side): they ask policy questions and get answers with citations.
